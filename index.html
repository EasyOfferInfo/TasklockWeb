TaskLock

TaskLock is a productivity app that helps users stay focused by restricting distracting apps until daily tasks or learning goals are completed.

Features

  • Block selected apps using Apple Screen Time
  • Unlock apps after completing tasks
  • Daily progress tracking
  • Vocabulary learning mode

Privacy

TaskLock does not collect personal data and only uses Apple Screen Time APIs locally on your device.
