WeEat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

WeEat ("we", "our", or "us") provides a private dinner-planning app that helps hosts create personal menus, organize gatherings, invite friends, and collect guests' dish selections before a meal.

Information We Collect

When you use WeEat, we may collect and store the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address when you sign in.
  • User-generated content, including menus, dishes, gathering information, guest selections, and photos you upload.
  • Technical information required to operate, secure, and maintain the app.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the WeEat service.
  • Allow you to create and manage personal menus.
  • Allow you to organize gatherings and invite guests.
  • Display dishes, photos, and guest selections within the app.
  • Improve app reliability, security, and performance.
  • Respond to customer support requests.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted service providers, such as cloud hosting, authentication, and storage providers, only as needed to operate the app.

User-Generated Content

WeEat allows users to create menus, add dishes, upload food-related photos, and organize private gatherings. Users are responsible for the content they create or upload. Inappropriate content may be removed.

Data Retention

We retain your information while your account remains active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

WeEat is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
