WeEat

WeEat is a private dinner-planning app that helps hosts share their personal menu, invite friends, and collect guests’ dish selections before a meal.

Features

  • Create and manage your personal menu
  • Organize private gatherings with friends and family
  • Let guests choose dishes before the meal
  • Track guest selections in one simple place
